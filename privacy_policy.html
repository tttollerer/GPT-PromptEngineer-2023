Privacy Policy for ChatGPT PromptEngineer 2023

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

ChatGPT PromptEngineer 2023 is a browser extension designed to enhance your experience on ChatGPT, Google Gemini (formerly Bard), and Bing Chat. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information processed by our extension.

Information Collection and Use

Our extension operates with a privacy-first approach:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or monitoring tools.
  • No External Servers: All extension functionality operates locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension uses browser local storage solely for:

  • Saving your language preferences
  • Storing custom prompt templates you create
  • Remembering your UI preferences (theme, collapsed sections)
  • Caching XML configuration files for performance

All stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To inject the prompt engineering interface into supported websites (ChatGPT, Gemini, Bing)
  • storage: To save your preferences and custom templates locally on your device

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. All interactions remain between your browser and the AI chat services you're already using (ChatGPT, Gemini, Bing).

Data Security

Since all data remains local to your browser:

  • Your data is as secure as your browser's local storage
  • No data transmission means no interception risks
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

Open Source

This extension is open source. You can review our code and verify our privacy practices on our GitHub repository.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the extension's privacy practices, please:

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Review the source code for transparency

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's no personal information to access, modify, or delete on our servers. All your data is stored locally and under your full control.
